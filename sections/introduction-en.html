Introduction back to ToC

The Norman Sicily Project attempts to record the Norman sites of Sicily. This vocabulary was developed to help describe them. It uses the concepts of Place which may or may not have a physical location and may or may not have a name. Currently, the vocabulary is in an early stage and provides classes and properties to describe monasteries.
